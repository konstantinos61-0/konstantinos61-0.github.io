Spring is a...season

Hello there, what's your favorite season? Mine is spring.

  • Nature is blooming with beautiful, colorful plans and flowers filling up the atmosphere with all sorts of refreshing fragrances.
  • The sun is shining!
  • the temperature is perfect for little trips to Nature and everything is perfect.

So i decide to step out my house and onto the fields i go. Fuck. My weak genes gave me allergies. My nose is itchy and I'm sneezing unstoppably while tears are running out of my eyes. In a desperate attempt for redemption I reach inside my bag for tissues only to find out i didn't bring any. I guess that's life.
